##DOM事件级别

- DOM0    ele.onclick = function(){}

    - `<button onclick="copyText()">Copy Text</button>`

- DOM2    ele.addEventListener('click', function(){}, false)

    - IE    attachEvent

- DOM3    ele.addEventListener('keyup', function(){}, false)

- DOM1标准制定时，没有涉及跟事件相关的
